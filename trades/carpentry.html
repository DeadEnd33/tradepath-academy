Carpenter

Creative • Physical • Skilled craftsmanship

What Carpenters Do

Carpenters build and repair structures using wood and other materials for homes and commercial buildings.

Salary Outlook

  • Entry Level: $32k–$42k
  • Experienced Carpenter: $50k–$70k
  • Contractor / Owner: $90k+

How to Get Started

  1. Apprenticeship or trade school
  2. Hands-on job training
  3. Specialize or start a business
