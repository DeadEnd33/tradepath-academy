Plumber

Essential work • Strong pay • Always in demand

What Plumbers Do

Plumbers install and repair water, drainage, and gas systems in residential and commercial buildings.

Salary Outlook

  • Apprentice: $38k–$48k
  • Journeyman: $55k–$75k
  • Master Plumber / Owner: $90k+

How to Get Started

  1. High school diploma or GED
  2. Trade school or apprenticeship
  3. Licensing exam
